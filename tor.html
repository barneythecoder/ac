Terms of Reward

  1. Principal driver (PD)of the winning vehicle will receive fuel vouchers equivalent to PHP300. The brand of the fuel shall be exclusively determined by AutoConscience (AC). The winner/s may request for diesel or gasoline.
  2. In cases where the PD of the winning vehicle is different from the registered owner of the vehicle then the vouchers will be split evenly between them.
  3. AC reserves the right to request proof that the individuals presenting themselves as the principal driver and/or registered owner indeed qualify as such. Determination of these qualification rests exclusively with AC.
  4. The date of posting the announcement of the winning vehicle on AC's FB page shall be deemed the date of posting. Rewards not claimed within 3 months of posting shall be deemed forfeited.
  5. A finder's reward shall be given to the person who accurately puts AC in touch with the PD and/or registered owner of the winning vehcile.
  6. AC shall exclusively determine who qualifies for the finder's reward.
  7. In cases where several individuals qualify for the finder's reward it will be split evenly between them.
