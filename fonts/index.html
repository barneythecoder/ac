Features

  • Easily report drivers

    • Report good and bad driver behaviour when you see one. Your reports are saved in the cloud and are immediately linked to the license plate you have entered. Strict filtering algorithms verify and give weight to reports you submit

  • Create your own personal account

    • Register and create your profile in AutoConscience. Link your license plate and see what your fellow drivers think of your driving!

  • Level up and earn rewards

    • Level-up as you drive! You gain levels and other rewards as you receive reports for your good driving. Stats are tracked and saved in our servers to tell you what kind of a driver you are

  • Make it personal!

    • Decorate your reports with fun stickers to truly express your gratitude (or anger) to the other driver. Driver let you in his lane? Send a thank you note. Another driver didn’t use turning signals? Send him poop (sticker, not an actual one)
